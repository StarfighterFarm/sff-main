Starfighter Products

Radish Microgreens

Starfighter Farm Radish Microgreens provide spice and heat to your meals! They can add heat without burning your insides later in the day like pepper oils would. Radish Microgreens can be eaten as a snack or to enhance numerous dishes from salads, omelets, cheeseburgers, and tacos to name a few.
